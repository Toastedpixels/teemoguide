Fundamentals

Common Beginner Mistakes

✕Stacking shrooms in the same spot — they don't stack, you're wasting charges
✕Dropping shrooms under a chaser — they need 1 second to arm, place them ahead
✕Fighting when Q is on cooldown vs auto-attack champs — back off and wait
✕Not using Q as an auto reset (auto → Q → auto = 3 hits instantly)
✕Grouping for teamfights mid/late — your job is usually to split push
✕Skipping Control Wards — they're cheap and destroy enemy vision + shrooms

Fight Combo — Step by Step

1 Check Q Is Q available? If yes, go fight. If not vs an auto-attack champ, wait for it to come back.
2 Stealth exit If in a bush, wait 1.5s for invisible passive — exit for the attack speed burst.
3 Auto → Q Land one auto first, then immediately Q — this resets your auto for a free extra hit!
4 Keep autoing 3 total hits triggers Press the Attack → they take 8% more damage from everything.
5 Evaluate Q back up? Stay and keep fighting. Q down vs auto-attacker? Kite back and poke from range.

Things People Get Wrong

·E is not an active ability — you can't press it. It's always-on poison on every auto attack.
·Shrooms need 1 second to arm — drop them under someone's feet and they walk right through.
·Q only blinds auto attacks — mages like Cassiopeia are not countered by your blind at all.
·Active W cannot be removed by damage — but the passive speed is lost when you get hit.
